    Features

    Handmade Art With Unique Designs

    All designs submitted are evaluated and I respond with feedback. I discuss the commission requirements and make suggestions for the piece. Once the design has been confirmed, I check in througout the painting process to ensure the customer is satisfied with the design progress.

    Premium Materials

    All paintings are made with vibrant paints, all paintings are varnished and all drawings are sprayed with a fixative. This is done in order to preserve the painting and ensure the work remains vibrant.

    Custom Commissions

    Any commission can be done with an inspired cartoon twist! From your favourite cartoon or anime series to cartoon portraiture.
